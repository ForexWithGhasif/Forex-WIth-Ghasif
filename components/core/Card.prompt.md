Translucent glass panel — the core surface for content blocks.

```jsx
<Card>Standard glass card</Card>
<Card interactive>Lifts and reveals a gold border on hover</Card>
<Card featured>Gold border + glow — the highlighted card in a set</Card>
```

Props: `featured` (gold treatment), `interactive` (hover lift), `padding`. Compose stats, headings, and buttons inside.
