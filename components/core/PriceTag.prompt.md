Live trading-pair row — pair, price, and signed change with a status dot.

```jsx
<PriceTag pair="EUR/USD" price="1.0942" change="+0.24%" direction="up" />
<PriceTag pair="GBP/JPY" price="188.41" change="-0.18%" direction="down" />
```

Numbers are tabular mono. `direction` colors the dot and delta emerald (up) or crimson (down). Stack several inside a Card for a watchlist.
