Small pill label for status, categories, or live trading deltas.

```jsx
<Badge tone="gold">VIP</Badge>
<Badge tone="bull" dot mono>↗ +612 pips</Badge>
<Badge tone="bear" mono>−1.4%</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral" dot>Live now</Badge>
<Badge tone="solid">Most popular</Badge>
```

Tones: `gold | bull | bear | neutral | solid`. `dot` adds a glowing status dot; `mono` switches to tabular figures for numbers.
