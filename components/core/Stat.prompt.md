Credibility metric block — big tabular number, label, optional delta.

```jsx
<Stat value="78.4%" label="Avg win rate" />
<Stat value="4,200+" label="Active members" delta="+318 this month" />
<Stat value="+612" label="Pips · 30 days" direction="up" align="center" />
```

Numbers render in tabular mono automatically. `delta` colors emerald (up) or crimson (down).
