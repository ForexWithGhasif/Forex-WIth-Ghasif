Standard section opener — gold eyebrow, display title, optional lead.

```jsx
<SectionHeading
  kicker="Why choose us"
  title="Discipline you can actually follow"
  lead="Every call comes with entry, stop, target, and the reasoning behind it."
/>
<SectionHeading align="center" kicker="Pricing" title="Choose your edge" />
```

Props: `kicker`, `title`, `lead`, `align` (`left`/`center`), `maxWidth`.
